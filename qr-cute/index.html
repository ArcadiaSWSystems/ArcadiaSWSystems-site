QR Cute

Make adorable QR codes, fast. Custom dot shapes, gradients, logos — codes that actually look good.

What it does

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

6 dot shapes Square, rounded, circle, diamond, heart, star — make it yours.
Custom colors & gradients Full color picker with gradient overlays for foreground and background.
Center logo Pick a built-in icon or upload your own image as a center logo.
7 outer shapes Heart, star, circle, squircle, bubble tea, coffee cup, and more.
Export as PNG or PDF 1024×1024 PNG for sharing, print-ready PDF for anything physical.
Encode anything URLs, text, email, phone, SMS, Wi-Fi credentials, vCard contacts.
Save your codes Name and save codes locally. One tap to restore any saved code with full styles.
iPhone, iPad & Mac Universal app. One purchase unlocks all your devices.
Zero data collection Everything stays on your device. No accounts, no tracking, no ads.
🔒

Your data never leaves your device.

QR Cute requires no account, collects no personal data, and sends nothing to any server. Everything is stored locally using Apple's SwiftData framework.
